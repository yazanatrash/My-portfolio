About Me

Dedicated Thermal-hydraulics and CFD engineer with more than a decade experience in performing thermal hydraulics analysis for nuclear power plants using both nuclear system codes such as RELAP5\MARS and CFD scale analysis codes. Received my Master and PhD degrees in nuclear engineering from the Korea Atomic Energy Research Institute (KAERI). During my PhD I contributed to the development of a state-of-art 3 dimensional CFD and components scale code called (CUPID). My main focus was on performing CFD scale investigation of sub-cooled flow boiling in nuclear power plant. In addition to my academic qualifications, I have two years on-site working experience at the Jordan Atomic Energy Commission (JAEC) as a nuclear safety analyst and as commissioning engineer for the Jordan Research and Training Reactor (JRTR), Jordan's first nuclear facility. Currently, I'm a senior consultant at the thermal hydraulics and safety team at NRG PALLAS helping to achieve Netherland goals for clean and independent energy.
